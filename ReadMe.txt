Project “Goal Post Pro” is an athletics based data gathering, storing and processing system/service that can allow sporting organizations of any/various disciplines to capture and store data relevant to their interests and then use that data to make informed decisions. Project “Goal Post Pro” should be a software solution that is first and foremost affordable for organizations operating in parts of the world where technical infrastructure is below that of the highest levels of sport and economies that have not prioritized sport in general (eg. Caribbean, parts of South America, parts of African Continent, parts of Asia etc)
